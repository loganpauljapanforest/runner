Title: Inevitable

Overview: An endless runner in the hellscape of a child's nightmare. Flee from a horrifying monster, avoid deadly obstacles, and do everything you can to put of the inevitable.

Instructions: Press space to jump, D to airdash, and S to slam towards the ground
